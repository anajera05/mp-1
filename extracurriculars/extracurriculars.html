Extracurriculars

BUFSA

BUFSA is an organization which advocates and celebrates the richness of the Filipino heritage! Through community involvement and education, BUFSA aims to expand our club's core values to the rest of BU students, faculty, staff, and alumni: diversity, inclusivity, acceptance, embracing and sharing Filipino culture. BUFSA is home for all students from all walks of life, and are always welcoming more members to join our family!

WiSCs

We are dedicated to creating a supportive community for young computer scientists of all genders and deepening awareness and interest in the many areas of CS. We feature speakers on a variety of CS topics, host career-related events, and bring together and collaborate with individuals passionate about computer science, math, engineering, business, and related fields. Computer science touches so many facets of our lives - from the virtual assistants on our smartphones to the algorithms running our medical equipment to the software that sends rockets into the stars - that it's impossible to not be "into computers." We hope that we can help all students, regardless of gender, to see that computer science can be for everyone - dreamers, tinkerers, coders, and thinkers - and to discover all that CS has to offer.
